In a forklift transport operation, incoming products are hauled from an arrival area to a storage area. This modeling problem has been posted by Noorjax Consulting as their AnyLogic Contest #2.

Our (non-spatial) model for this problem, called Forklift-Transport-1, is described by the DPMN process diagram to the right.

Default scenario

The default scenario has 4 forklifts and 6 operators.
